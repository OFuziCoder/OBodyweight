Gives every NPC a unique body automatically — no BodySlide presets to collect. A whole town of different shapes, with physics that fit each body.


==================================================================
DESCRIPTION
==================================================================
[size=6][b]OBodyNG Weight[/b][/size]

[i]Every NPC gets their own body — automatically.[/i]

Walk into a town and no two people share the same figure. Slim, curvy, athletic, petite, heavy — each NPC is built for you on the spot, [b]without collecting or sorting a single BodySlide preset[/b]. Their clothes fit the body too, and they keep the same look every time you meet them.

It just works in the background. Install it, and the world fills with believable, varied bodies — men and women.


==================================================================
MAIN FEATURES
==================================================================
[list]
[*][b]No preset library needed[/b] — you don't download, sort, or assign body presets. Every body is made automatically, for women and men.
[*][b]Three ways to build bodies[/b] — fully procedural; or OBody's own presets but varied per NPC (no more identical twins sharing a preset); or a blend of the two. Switch in the menu.
[*][b]15 body types[/b] — each woman is one recognisable shape: balanced, slim, rectangle, pear, top-heavy, hourglass, voluptuous, soft, BBW, athletic, athletic-curvy, obese, stocky, petite or amazon. Bust, waist, hips and tone all fit the type — not random parts thrown together.
[*][b]Mostly natural, with variety[/b] — most people look average and believable; the striking bodies (tiny, huge, very curvy) are the exception, the way a real crowd looks. You're not surrounded by clones or by nothing but bombshells.
[*][b]Physics that fit the body[/b] — with CBPC installed, each body moves to match: soft and heavy bodies jiggle more, toned and lean ones stay firm, and collision scales with size. Optional — without CBPC nothing changes.
[*][b]Clothes follow the body[/b] — armor and outfits fit the generated shape, with no neck seams or body-vs-clothes mismatch.
[*][b]Muscle tone[/b] — some women are athletic, with real definition, up to a rare super-fit "amazon". Men get tone from their build. A softer body smooths it away naturally.
[*][b]Per-sex switches[/b] — turn female and male bodies on or off independently (let OBody/vanilla handle a sex), plus a slider to make men slimmer or beefier to taste.
[*][b]Re-roll hotkey[/b] — aim at anyone and press a key for a brand-new body on the spot (re-bindable). Works in VR (it uses where you're looking).
[*][b]Refresh everyone[/b] — a one-click "Reprocess all loaded NPCs" button in the menu re-applies everything to the people around you after you change a setting — no waiting, no reloading.
[*][b]Light and tidy[/b] — easy on performance, and it doesn't take up a load-order slot. Works on Skyrim SE, AE and VR.
[/list]

[b]Settings menu (MCM):[/b] how varied or exaggerated bodies are, how common athletic / unusual bodies are, men on/off and male build, a re-bindable re-roll key, and a button to refresh everyone around you. A FOMOD installer offers Realistic / Balanced / Fantasy starting presets — all adjustable later.


==================================================================
INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS
==================================================================
[list=1]
[*]Install with a mod manager (MO2 / Vortex) and enable [b]OBodyNGWeight.esp[/b], loaded after OBody NG.
[*]Pick a starting flavor in the installer — Realistic, Balanced, or Fantasy (you can change it anytime in the menu).
[*]In-game, open the "OBodyNG Weight" menu and choose the [b]Procedural[/b] body mode.
[*]Recommended: in OBody's own menu, set its NPC-selection hotkey to [b]None[/b], so the re-roll key only triggers this mod.
[/list]
Make sure your CBBE / 3BA (female) and HIMBO (male) bodies are built in BodySlide with [b]"Build Morphs" checked[/b]. Build your armors with morphs too if you want clothing to follow the body.
